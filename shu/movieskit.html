Movie Skit

Part 1

  • Make a group of 3 to 5 people.
  • As a group, choose a movie. Then, write a screenplay for a new scene that takes place (happened) one year after the movie ends (or alternatively, before the movie begins).
  • Each group will write one screenplay.
  • The final screenplay must be typed (due week 17).
  • A first draft of your screenplay should be ready by week 16.

Part 2

  • You will perform your scene in front of the class.
  • It will be done on week 17.
  • The performance should be between 3-5 minutes.
  • Generally one page of a screenplay is one minute. So your screenplays should be between 3-5 pages in length.
  • You will turn in the final draft of your screenplay before you go on stage.

Other resources concerning screenplays:

Wikipedia: a wiki page for screenplays.

IMSDB: The International Movie Script Database, where thre are many examples of actual movie screenplays, however they are usually not the final drafts.

Writers Store: A somewhat more complicated description of how to write a screenplay, including some details about things like voice overs, and similar things.
