Final Project Fall 2019

Make a group with 3-4 students.

As a group, write a dialogue. Make sure you have at least 6 lines of dialogue per person. It can be on any topic. Try to use vocabulary from one (or more) of the units in your book. (Units 1, 2, 4, and 5)

Use props to get extra points.

You will perform your dialogue in front of the class in two weeks.
