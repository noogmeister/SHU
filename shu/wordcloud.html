Spring 2019 Word Cloud Project

Step 1: General Instructions

  1. Watch the video on success (at the bottome of the page. Watch it once without subtitles (or with English subtitles if you want), then go to settings and turn on the Chinese subtitles and watch it again.
  2. Go to the school's e-lean site and do the assignment concerning this video (week 4). Do this before you look at the project. Give yourself some time to think about the ideas in the video.
  3. After you have watched the video and completed the assignment above, I want you to think about someone who has inspired you in life. This person CAN NOT be a family member.
  4. Brainstorm some ideas about this person. Just make a list at this stage. Who is he or she? How have they become successful? (use ideas from the video). How did they affect your life? I suggest you spend one day just doing this.
  5. Now the hard part. Write a 300 to 500 word essay about this person who has inpired you. Be sure to tell me why they have inpired you, and who this person is.
  6. After you have written your paper introducing someone who has inpired you in your life you will then make a word cloud (Yes!) using the words in your paper, and the text from a wikipedia site related to the person you are writing about, or their profession.
  7. For example, if your English teacher at SHU has inspired you, you will use the words from your paper, and the words from wikipedia concerning English Teaching.
  8. Another example would be, say, Lady Gaga has inspired you. Then you would use the words from your paper and the wikipedia article about Lady Gago to make the word cloud.

Step 2: Creating the Word Cloud

  1. Go to the website wordclouds.
    1. Size: choose (4:3)HD which is the default.
    2. Note: It is very important that you do not include any personal information in your word cloud. So be careful of this when you copy text into the word list.
    3. Word List: at the top click 'Paste/Type text' (you can find this under File as well) and copy and paste the text from your story and click 'Apply.'
      1. Some of the most common words may not show in your word cloud, so you can lower the number in the word list if you need to.
    4. Gap Size: choose 4 to 6 or whatever you think looks best.
    5. Direction of words: choose whatever you want.
    6. Invert: use this if you want.
    7. Shape: this is very important. Choose one of the shapes they have, or add your own using 'upload.'
    8. Colors / Theme : choose the background and cloud colors you want. Or go with a theme.
    9. File: when you are finished, save your word cloud as a PNG file. The filename should be your *student number* and *English name* written together. Example: a106999123mark.png

      10. Finally, you will upload both your word cloud and your writing using the Google Forms links below.

Step 3: Uploading the PNG Image and Writing Files

Due Date: Before your class time on week 8 (The midterm week)

  1. Go to classes and click on the link undser your class time. It is important that you choose the correct class time, so please don't click on the wrong class!
    1. Follow the instructions to upload your word cloud file using the correct google form link.
