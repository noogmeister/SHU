Notes for Fall 2019

Week 2

Vocabulary (not in the book)

  1. Types of Communication
    1. oral: communicating with your mouth (and ears) using language. Talking, having a conversation, giving a speech, etc.
    2. written: communicating through writing. Letters, books, texting.
    3. non-verbal: body language. Communicating through gestures, eye contact, and facial expressions.
    4. visual: communicating with images, colors, signs.
  2. Syllabus: course outline and schedule, and code of conduct. Also explains how grades will be determined.
    1. attendance failure: failing because you don't come to class. "KK."
    2. participation: asking and answering questions, participating in in-class activities. Taking quizes.
    3. personal day: not coming to class for personal reasons, as opposed to being sick, or other school recognized absences. This is really just to emphasize that it doesn't matter why you are absent.

We also watched the short film Signs and discussed types of communication.
