Movie Presentation

Create a group of 3-4 students.
As a group create a powerpoint (.ppt) presentation about a movie that interests you.
Each person should talk about one aspect of the movie.
Each person should talk for about one minute. Some possible aspects are:

  • Characters in the film
  • Plot(story) of the film
  • Direction (look, cinematography, style) of the film
  • Personal reaction to the film

The presentation should be between 3-5 minutes in length.
No Trailers (Videos!) because you will go overtime.
There is a limit of 1 movie per class. So, for example, I don't want to see 6 presentations on Titanic.
