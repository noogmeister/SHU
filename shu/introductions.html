Introductions:

  1. Choose a person who has inspired you in your life and give a 2-3 minute speech about this person.
  2. Make sure to tell us what he or she does and why they have inspired you.
  3. Do not choose someone who is related to you. This means no family membors!
  4. You will not use any kind of visual aids for this presentation
  5. You may have note cards such as 3x7 or similar cards
