Event Poster

Create a group with 2 or 3 people.
Together draw a picture advertising a future event (such as on the top of page 17 in your textbook). You will need to promote it in class. You should talk about it for 2-3 minutes (about 1 minute per person).

Events include:

  • A movie
  • A concert
  • A talk
  • A play
  • An art exhibit

When you talk about your poster, please include the following information:

  • What the event is.
  • Why we should go and see it.
  • Where the event is.
  • When the event takes place.
  • How much it costs, if anything.

This project is due on April 9, 2019.
