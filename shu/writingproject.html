Self-Introduction Writing Project

Write a 300-400 word self-introduction paper telling me about yourself.

Please include the following:

  1. Where you are from.
  2. Who is in your family.
  3. What you are studying a Shih Hsin University.
  4. What your hobbies or special interests are.

The paper should be written in paragraph format. And please include a topic sentence in each paragraph. We will discuss these things in class.
You will have two weeks to work on this project.

Please write your personal information at the top your paper. The information should include:

  1. Your English Name.
  2. Your Student Number.
  3. Your Class Time.
  4. Your Seat Number.

You will be required to upload your project using a google form. Please provide all the information it asks for.
