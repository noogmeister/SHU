Project Information

  • Creat a group of 3 or 4 students.
  • As a group, create a powerpoint presentation introducing the class to an upcomimg event (future event).
  • example of events are:
    • A movie premier
    • A concert
    • A play
    • An art exhibition
    • A talk or lecture
    • A festival
  • Be sure to tell us what the event is, where the venue is, when it will take place, and, most importantly, why we should go and see it.
  • The presentations should be around 5 mintues.
  • Everyone in the group should talk for at least 1 minute.
  • You will do your presentations as per the revised schedule I handed out in class.
