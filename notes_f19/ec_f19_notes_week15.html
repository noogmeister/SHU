Notes for English Conversation class, week 15-16, Fall 2019

Week 15

Music Quiz

The quiz will be about the following topics:

-- music genres

-- issues discussed in the songs (feminism, sexism, drug abuse, codependency, irony)

-- style (mashup, looping, backmasking, strong bass)

-- pop music and Sweden

-- Alternative Rock and why it is called alternative.

-- the origin of certain genres of music (folk > country, blues > rock)
