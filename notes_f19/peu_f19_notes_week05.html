Discussion questions for the movie I Am Legend:

Why is the film called "I Am Legend?" Why is Neville a legend?

What role does music play in the film? Why is it important? Who is the artist?

Why is the symbol of the butterfly important? What does it mean?

Why is the woman (and the boy) in the film?

What does the term deus ex machina mean? Does it apply to this film?

What is the genre of this film?
