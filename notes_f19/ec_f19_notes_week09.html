Some comprehension questions concerning Source Code for discussion next week:

When Captain Colter wakes up on a train he is someone else. What is the name of the person? What does this person do?

Why does communications officer Goodwin ask Colter do things like name card suits in descending order?

Where does Colter find the bomb? Does he disarm the bomb or leave it undisturbed? After that, what does he ask the passengers on the train to do?

What was the last thing Colter remembers before waking up on the train?

Who does Colter follow off of the train? Who does he take with him when he leaves? Why do you think he decides to follow the man? How does he die after fighting with the man over a briefcase?

How does to movie end? Does he 'fix' the timeline, or create a new one?
